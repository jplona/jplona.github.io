QUICHE LORRAINE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
QUICHE
Preparation time
1 hr
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 1 Pie Crust for 9" Pie
  • 1/2# Bacon, fried & crumbled
  • 1 cup Swiss Cheese, shredded
  • 1/3 cup Onion, minced
  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 cups Light Cream
  • 3/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Sugar
  • 1/8 tsp Cayenne Pepper

Instructions

Heat oven 425F Line prepared crust into 9" pie dish. Sprinkle with bacon, cheese & onion. With electric beater, blend remaining ingredients. Pour into crust. Bake 15 min. Reduce temp. to 300F. Bake 40 min longer or until knife inserted 1 " from edge c omes out clean. Let pie stand 10-15 min before cutting.
