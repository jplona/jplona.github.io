ZUCCHINI & SAUSAGE QUICHE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
QUICHE
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 1 10" unbaked Pie Shell
  • 2 cups Zucchini, shred & unpeeled
  • 1 tbsp Butter or Margarine
  • 1/2 # Bulk Pork Sausage ------------
  • browned & well drained
  • 1 cup Swiss Cheese, shredded
  • 4 Eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1/2 cup Half & Half Cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Pepper

Instructions

Preheat oven to 450F Bake the pie shell for 8 min Saute zucchini in butter until tender. Drain. Spread cooked zucchini on bottom of pastry shell, sprinkle with crumbled cooked sausage & then sprinkle Swiss cheese over the top. Combine eggs, milk, crea m, Parmesan cheese & seasonings, mixing well. Pour into shell & bake at 450F for 15 min. Lower temp to 350F & bake until center is firm but still soft, about 20 to 30 min.
