HAM & CHEESE QUICHE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
QUICHE
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Ham, cubed
  • 1/2 # Bacon, cook, dice & drain
  • 1 cup Cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup Colby-Jack cheese, shredded
  • 1 sml Onion
  • 4 oz Mushrooms,slice & sautee
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 4 Eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup Biscuit baking mix
  • Salt & Pepper, to taste

Instructions

In greased deep 9" pie dish layer ham, bacon, cheeses, onion & mushrooms. Set aside. In medium bowl, combine eggs, milk, biscuit mix, salt & pepper. Beat well. Pour over ham & cheese mixture. Bake in preheated oven 10 min @ 400F. Reduce temp. to 350F & b ake 40 - 45 min.
