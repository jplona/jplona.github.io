DROP BISCUITS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
BREADS
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
2 doz

Ingredients

  • 6 3/4 cup Unbleached Flour
  • 3 Tbsp Baking Powder
  • 3 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Kosher Salt
  • 2 Sticks + 5 Tbsp cold Butter,chop
  • 2 1/2 cup Buttermilk

Instructions

Work with hands to oatmeal consistancy. Whisk all ingredients. Brush with egg wash. ( 1 beaten egg with 1 Tbsp milk ) Bake 425 F for 10-12 min. Suggestion: Divide dough in half. Add chives to make 1 dozen.
