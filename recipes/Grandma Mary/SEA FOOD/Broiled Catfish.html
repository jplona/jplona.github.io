BROILED CATFISH

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SEAFOOD
Preparation time
15 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 6 Catfish Fillets or Whole Fish
  • 1/3 cup Soy Sauce
  • 3 tbsp Oil
  • 1 clove Garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp Ginger, optional
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1/4 tsp White Pepper

Instructions

Clean, wash & dry fish. Combine remaining ingredients except lemon wedges & mix thoroughly. Place fish on a well-greased broiler pan; brush with sauce. Broil about 3 inches from source of heat for 4 to 6 min. Turn carefully & brush other side with sa uce. Broil 4 to 6 min longer, basting occasionally, until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork. Serve with lemon wedges.
