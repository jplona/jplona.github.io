SOLE (One Pan Dish)

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SEA FOOD
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
4

Ingredients

  • 4 fillets of Sole, 4 - 6 oz each
  • 7 tbsp Butter
  • 1 1/2 cups Mushrooms, fresh & slice
  • 2 tbsp Parsley, minced
  • 1 cup Breadcrumbs, fresh
  • 2 tbsp Shallots, minced
  • Salt & White Pepper, to taste

Instructions

Preheat oven 350F. Using 4 tbsp butter, grease baking dish. Scatter shallots in dish. Lay fish atop shallots, top with mushrooms, salt, pepper & parsley. Dust with breadcrumbs. Using 3 tbsp butter, dot surface with small pieces. Bake for 10 - 12 min.
