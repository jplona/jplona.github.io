CRAB SALAD

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SEAFOOD
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 10 Imitation Crab Legs
  • 1/2 Head Shredded Lettuce
  • 3 Ribs Celery, diced
  • 2 Eggs, hard-boilled & chopped
  • 5 tbsp Mayonaise
  • Lowry Seasoning, optional

Instructions

Toss all ingredients together lightly.
