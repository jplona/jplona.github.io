HADDOCK & HERBS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SEAFOOD
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 2 # Haddock Fillets
  • 1 can (1 lb) Tomatoes
  • 1 Clove Garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Salad Oil
  • 1 tsp Parsley Flakes
  • 1/4 tsp Oregano, dried
  • 1/4 tsp Thyme, dried

Instructions

Place fish in buttered 11 3/4 x 7 1/2 x 1 3/4 baking dish. Combine remaining ingredients; pour over fish. Bake , basting frequently, in 350F oven about 20 - 30 min or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.
