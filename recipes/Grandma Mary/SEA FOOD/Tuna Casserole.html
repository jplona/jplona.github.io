TUNA CASSEROLE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SEAFOOD
Preparation time
30 min.
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Celery, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp. Butter or Margarine
  • (1) 6 1/2-oz can Tuna, undrained
  • (1) 10 1/2-oz can Mushroom Soup
  • 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 4-oz can Sliced Mushrooms, drained
  • 1 tbsp. Pimento, chopped
  • 8-oz Wide Noodles, cooked
  • 1/2 cup Bread Crumbs
  • 2 tbsp Butter or Margarine, melted

Instructions

In large skillet saute onion & celery in butter or margarine. Add & mix tuna, soup, mayonnaise, milk, mushrooms, & pimento. In a greased 2 quart casserole, combine noodles and tuna mixture. Combine bread crumbs & 2 tbsp melted butter & sprinkle on top. Bake at 350 F. for 25 to 30 min. or until browned & bubbly.
