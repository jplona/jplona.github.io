CHEESE CAKE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
CAKE
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
12

Ingredients

  • 24 oz Philadelphia Cr. Cheese
  • 7 - 8 Eggs
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 1/3 tsp Vanilla
  • CRUST
  •   1 1/2 CUP Graham Cracker Crumbs
  •   1/2 stick Butter
  •   2 tbsp Sugar
  • TOPPING
  •   1 1/2 pints Sour Cream
  •   6 tbsp Sugar
  •   1 1/2 tsp Vanilla

Instructions

Cream the cheese. Add eggs one at a time. Add sugar & vanilla. Prepare crust. Line bottom & 1/2" up sides of a 9 x 13 " pan. Pour mixture into crust. Bake 325F for 40 min. Remove from oven Spread on topping. Bake 475F for 5 - 10 min.
