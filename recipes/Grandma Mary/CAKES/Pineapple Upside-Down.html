PINEAPPLE UPSIDE-DOWN CAKE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
CAKE
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
10

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg Duncan Hines Moist Deluxe
  • Pineapple Supreme Cake Mix
  • 1/2 cup Butter or Margarine
  • 1 cup firmly packed Brown Sugar
  • 20 oz Can Pineapple slices, drained
  • Maraschino Cherries, drain & halve

  • Duncan Hines Moist Deluxe Yellow Cake Mix may also be used

Instructions

MELT butter in ungreased 13x9" pan. SPRINKLE brown sugar evenly in pan. ARRANGE pineapple on brown sugar. PLACE cherry halves, cut side up, in center of pineapple slices. PREPARE cake mix per directions for Basic Recipe. POUR batter evenly over fruit in pan. BAKE @ 350 F for 40 min or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. COOL 5 min in pan. INVERT onto plate.
