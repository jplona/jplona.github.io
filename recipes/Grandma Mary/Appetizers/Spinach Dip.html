SPINACH DIP

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
APPETIZER
Preparation time
15 min
Servings
8 - 10

Ingredients

  • 1 envelope Lipton Veg. Soup Mix
  • 8 oz Sour Cream, light or reg.
  • 1 cup Mayo, light or reg.
  • 10 oz pkg. frozen Spinach, chopped
  • 8 oz water Chestnuts, drain & chop

Instructions

In medium bowl, combine all ingredients. Chill at least 2 hours. Serve with your favorite dippers.
