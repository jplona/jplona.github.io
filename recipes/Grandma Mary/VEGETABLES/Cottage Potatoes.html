COTTAGE POTATOES

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLES
Preparation time
30 Min.
Servings
8 to 10

Ingredients

  • 10 med Red Potatoes, cook & cube
  • 1 medium Green Pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium Onion, chopped
  • 1/2# Velvetta Cheese, cubed
  • 2 slices White Bread, cubed
  • 1 cup melted Butter, or less
  • 2 tbsp Red Bell Pepper, chopped

Instructions

Layer potatoes, green peppers, red peppers, onions, & cheese. Pour melted butter over entire surface. Distribute cubed bread on top & bake at 350 F for 45 minutes in greased casserole dish.
