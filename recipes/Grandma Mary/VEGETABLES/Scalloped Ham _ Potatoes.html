SCALLOPED HAM & POTATOES

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLES
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 4 cups heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 cloves Garlic, slivered
  • 1 tsp dried Thyme leaves
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper
  • 1 # Ham, sliced
  • 2 # Potatoes, peeled & thinly sliced
  • 1 lg Onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp fresh Parsley, chopped

Instructions

Combine cream, garlic, thyme, salt & pepper in heavy saucepan. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer & cook 10 to 12 min until sauce has reduced by third. Butter shallow 8 x 14 pyrex dish. Layer 1/2 of ham, 1/2 of potatoes, 1/2 of onion .Pour 1/2 of cream mi xture over layers. Repeat layering. Bake 350F for 1 hr until brown & bubbly.
