RED POTATOES & ROSEMARY

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLES
Preparation time
15 min
Servings
6 - 8

Ingredients

  • 3 # Sml Red Potatoes
  • 1/3 cup Olive Oil
  • 3 cloves Garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp Rosemary Leaves, dried OR
  • 2 tbsp Rosemary, fresh
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese, grated

Instructions

Heat oven to 375F. Halve or quarter potatoes. Add remaining ingredients. Bake 30 to 40 min or until done.
