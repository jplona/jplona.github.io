CREOLE CAULIFLOWER

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLE
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Green Pepper, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Butter or Margarine
  • 3 tbsp Flour
  • 3 fresh medium Tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/8 tsp Pepper
  • 3 cups Cauliflowerets, cooked
  • Buttered Toast

Instructions

Saute onion & green pepper in butter or margarine until lightly browned, 10 min. Blend in flour. Add tomatoes, salt & pepper. Stir & cook until thickened. Add cauliflower. Serve on buttered toast.
