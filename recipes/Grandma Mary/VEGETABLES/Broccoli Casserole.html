BROCCOLI CASSEROLE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLES
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
10

Ingredients

  • 1 # bag plus 10 oz box frozen
  • chopped Broccoli, cooked slightly
  • & drained
  • 1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 cup Cheddar Cheese, grated
  • 1 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup Onion, coarsely chopped
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

Place broccoli in a 13" x 9" baking pan. Combine remaining ingredients in a blender until creamy. Pour over broccoli & mix well. Bake in preheated 350 F oven for 40 to 45 minutes until set.
