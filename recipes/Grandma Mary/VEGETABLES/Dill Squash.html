DILL SQUASH

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLE
Preparation time
15 min
Servings
2 - 3

Ingredients

  • 1 medium Yellow Squash
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Parsley, fresh & snipped
  • 1/4 tsp Dill Weed
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • Dash of Onion Powder

Instructions

Cut squash into slices 1/4" thick. Melt butter in skillet. Combine all ingredients. Cover & cook over low heat for 8 to 10 min or until tender. Stir occasionally. Serve immediately.
