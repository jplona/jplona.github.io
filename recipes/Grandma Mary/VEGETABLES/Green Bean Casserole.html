GREEN BEAN CASSEROLE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLES
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 1 can Cr Mushroom Soup (10 3/4 oz)
  • 1/2 cup Milk`
  • 4 cups Green Beans, cooked
  • 1 1/3 cup Fr Fried Onions, (2.8 oz)
  • 1 tsp Soy Sauce, optional
  • Dash Pepper

Instructions

In 1 1/2 qt casserole mix soup, milk, soy, pepper, beans & 2/3 cup onions. Bake 350F for 25 min or until hot. Stir. Sprinkle remaining 2/3 cup onions over bean mixture. Bake 5 min or until onions are golden.
