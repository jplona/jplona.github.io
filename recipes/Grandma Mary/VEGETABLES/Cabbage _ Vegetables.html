Cabbage & Vegetables

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLE
Preparation time
15 min.
Servings
6-8

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Cabbage
  • 1 cup Celery
  • 1 cup Green Pepper
  • 1/4 cup Onion, chopped
  • 1 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tbsp Bacon Drippings
  • 2 tbsp Water
  • Pepper to Taste

Instructions

Combine ingredients and cook about 10 min. Good with Ham or Pork.
