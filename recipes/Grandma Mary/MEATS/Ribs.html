RIBS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
MEAT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
4

Ingredients

  • 6 to 8# Spare Ribs
  • 2 tsp Cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 8 1/2 oz Can Crushed Pineapple
  • 3 tbsp Honey
  • 2 tbsp Cider Vinegar

Instructions

Combine pineapple & cornstarch. Add honey, soy sauce, & vinegar. Heat 5 min. Steam ribs in heavy double thickness alumifoil with 2 tbsp water. Cook on grill for 1 hr. Do not open packet while steaming. Remove ribs & place over heat generously basting wit h sauce. Cook another 30 min, 15 on each side, being careful not to burn sauce.
