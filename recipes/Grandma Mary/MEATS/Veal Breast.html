VEAL BREAST - STUFFED

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
MEAT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
4 - 6

Ingredients

  • 4 Rib Veal Breast
  • 1/2# Chicken Livers
  • 1/2 cup Onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Celery, chopped
  • 4 tbsp Butter
  • 1 cup Seasoned Bread Stuffing
  • 1/2 cup Parsley, chopped
  • 2 Eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup MIlk

Instructions

Trim Breast of Veal. Slice a big pocket along top portion of rib bones. Saute 1/2# chicken livers in 2 tbsp butter. Chop finely. Set aside. Saute onion & celery in 2 tbsp butter until soft. Combine livers, vegetables, bread stuffing, parsley, salt & pe pper. Add milk. Add eggs. Stuff veal to thickness of 1" across breast. Skewer breast with 2 wooden skewers. Roast 2 to 2 1/2 hrs @ 350F.
