CROCKPOT BEEF

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
MEAT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
4 - 6

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 to 2 # lean Beef Chuck, cubed
  • 2 Medium Onions, quartered
  • 4 Celery Stalks, bite size pcs
  • 4 Carrots, bite size pcs
  • 1 Green Pepper, bite size pcs
  • 1 # Mushrooms or 1 jar, drained
  • 16oz can Whole Tomatoes & juice
  • OR 1 can diced Tomatoes & juice
  • 3 tbsp Molasses, 3 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Gravy Master
  • 1/2 cup Flour
  • Pepper to taste

Instructions

Place meat, onions, celery, carrots, peppers, and mushrooms in crockpot. Add flour & mix to coat thoroughly. Add tomatoes with juice, pepper, molasses, Gravy Master & cook on low setting for 8 hours ( or 4 hours on high setting) Serve over steamed rice or lightly seasoned egg noodles
