LAMB SHANKS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
MEAT
Preparation time
25 min
Servings
4

Ingredients

  • 4 Lamb Shanks (3 1/2 to 4#)
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 2 tbsp Onions, diced
  • 1/3 tsp Pepper
  • 3 cup Diced Vegetables - - - -
  • Onions, Carrots, Celery,
  • Turnips & Tomatoes, Grn Peppers
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 1/2 cup Tomato Juice
  • 1/2 Cup Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 Bay Leaf
  • 3 tbsp Flour

Instructions

Rub lamb shanks with sliced garlic. Coat well with flour. In a heavy dutch oven, heat oil. Sear lamb shanks in hot oil. Add onions & cook until meat is well browned on all sides. Pour off fat. Add tomato juice, pepper & bay leaf. Cover & simmer (or bake at 325F) for 1 1/2 hrs or until lamb shanks are fork tender. Add vegetables & 1 /2 cup water. Continue to simmer until vegetables are tender, 30 min.
