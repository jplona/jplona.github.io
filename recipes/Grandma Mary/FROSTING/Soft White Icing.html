SOFT WHITE ICING

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
FROSTING
Preparation time
15 min
Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg White
  • 1 cup Confectioners' Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Crisco
  • 1 tbsp Margarine
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Confectioners' Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Milk
  • 2 tsp Vanilla

Instructions

Beat Egg white until stiff; add one cup confectioners' sugar one tbsp at a time. Beat until stiff & set aside. Cream crisco with the one tablespoon margarine, salt, confectioners' sugar, flour, milk & vanilla. When beaten very light, add the egg white mixture and beat until smooth & creamy. Excellent on any cake, especially on Devil's Food!
