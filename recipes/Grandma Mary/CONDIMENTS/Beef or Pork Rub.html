BEEF OR PORK RUB

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
CONDIMENTS
Preparation time
15 min
Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Fennel Seed, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp Thyme
  • 2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 tsp Salt

Instructions

Combine ingredients. Wash meat & pat dry. Lightly oil surface of meat. Sprinkle 1 to 2 tbsp, to taste, of rub per pound of meat & rub in. Let stand for 1 hr or more in the refrigerator before cooking.
