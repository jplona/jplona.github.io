TANGY BARBECUE SAUCE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
CONDIMENT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
2 qts

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Pickling Spice
  • 1 Qt Cider Vinegar
  • 2 cups Water
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 15 oz Tomato Sauce
  • 14 oz bottle Catsup 1/4 tsp Hot Pepper
  • 12 oz can Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar, packed
  • 1/2 cup Butter or Margarine
  • 1/4 cup Lemon Juice
  • 1 Large Onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce

Instructions

1 tbsp Salt, 1 tbsp Chili Powder, 1 tbsp Garlic Powder, 1 tbsp Crushed Red Pepper Flakes, & 1/4 tsp Hot Pepper Sauce. INSTRUCTIONS Measure pickl ing spice into small square of cheesecloth, gather cheesecloth around spice & tie ends together with string. Place in large kettle; add remaining ingredients & simmer 1 1/2 hrs. Remove cheesecloth. Store sauce, tightly covered, in refrigerator.
