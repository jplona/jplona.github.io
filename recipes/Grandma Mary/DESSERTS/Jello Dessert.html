JELLO DESSERT

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
12

Ingredients

  • 3 Egg Yolks
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Melted Butter
  • 20 oz Crushed Pineapple, drained
  • 3 Egg Whites
  • 1/2 cup Walnuts, chopped
  • Graham Crackers
  • 2 sml or 1 lg box Jello, Cherry or
  • Strawberry

Instructions

Prepare Jello. Let partially set. Beat egg yolks with sugar, add melted butter; mix. Add pineapple & nuts. Beat egg whites until stiff & blend in. Line a 9 x 13" dish with graham crackers. Pour 1/2 of mixture over crackers. Add another layer of cr ackers & pour remaining mixture. Add layer of crackers; pour partially set jello. Refrigerate until jello is completely set. Top with whipping cream.
