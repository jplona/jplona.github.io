CHERRY ANGEL DESSERT

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
10

Ingredients

  • 8 cup Angel Food Cake (small)
  • 1 can Cherry Pie Filling
  • 1 box Vanilla Instant Pudding
  • 1 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1 cup Sour Cream

Instructions

In 9 x 9 x 2 pan spread 1/2 cubed cake . Spoon cherry pie filling on cake. Spread remainder of cubed cake. Combine pudding mix, milk & sour cream. Beat until smooth. Pour over cake. Chill at least 5 hours or over night.
