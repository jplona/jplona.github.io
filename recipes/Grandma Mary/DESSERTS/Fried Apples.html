FRIED APPLES

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
15

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Flour
  • 1 1/2 rounded tsp Baking Powder
  • 5/8 cup cold Water
  • 1/8 cup Milk
  • Pinch Sugar
  • 3 Granny Smith or Jonathan Apples
  • 1/2 cup Vegetable Oil

Instructions

Combine flour, baking powder, water, milk & sugar in small bowl & set aside. Core & slice apples crosswise 1/8" thick, leaving skins on. Dip each slice in batter & tap against side of bowl to remove excess. Heat oil in 12" skillet until very hot. Add slices, being careful not to crowd skillet. Brown on both sides & drain on paper towel. Serve immediately
