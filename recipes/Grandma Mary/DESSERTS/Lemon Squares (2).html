LEMON SQUARES (2)

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
18 pcs

Ingredients

  • CRUST
  • 1 stick Margarine
  • 1 cup Flour
  • 1/4 cup Conf. Sugar
  • Mix as for pie crust
  • FILLING
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 3 tbsp Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Lemon zest
  • Mix above ingredients with FORK

Instructions

Pat crust mixture into 8 x 12" pyrex dish, building up sides about 1/4". Bake 350F, 15-20 min. Add filling & bake 15 min more until filling is done. A crust will form when done. Sprinkle with conf. sugar. Cut while warm & let cool in pan.
