LEMON SQUARES

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
14

Ingredients

  • FILLING:
  • 2 boxes Lemon Pie Filling
  • 4 tbsp Sugar
  • CRUST:
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 2 sticks Margarine, softened
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • LAYER INGREDIENTS:
  • 12 oz Cream Cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups Conf. Sugar
  • 8 oz Cool Whip

Instructions

Cook filling as directed. Mix crust ingredients and press into 10 x 15 cookie sheet. Bake 15 to 20 min at 350 F. Cool crust completely. Beat 12 oz cream cheese, 1 1/2 cups conf. sugar until smooth. Spread on cooled curst, the cooler the better. Add cool pie filling on top. Add cool whip.
