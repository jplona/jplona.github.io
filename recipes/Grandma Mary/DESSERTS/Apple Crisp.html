APPLE CRISP

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6 - 8

Ingredients

  • 6 cups sliced peeled Apples
  • 1/4 cup Sugar or Honey
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 2 tbsp Butter or Margarine, melted
  • 3 tbsp Butter or Margarine
  • 1/3 cup Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Flour
  • 1 1/2 cups Post Raisin Bran

Instructions

Mix apples with 1/4 cup sugar, cinnamon & salt. Stir in melted butter. Spoon into 1 1/2 qt shallow baking dish. Cream butter, blend in 1/3 cup sugar & the flour. Stir in cereal; sprinkle over apple mixture. Cover; bake at 350 F for 30 min. Uncover; b ake 10 to 15 min longer, until apples are tender. Serve warm with whipped topping, if desired.
