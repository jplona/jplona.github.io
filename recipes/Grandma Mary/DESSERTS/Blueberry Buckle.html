BLUEBERRY BUCKLE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
9

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup soft Butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 1/4 cups Flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp grated Lemon Zest
  • 1//3 cup Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups Blueberries, fresh
  • Crumb Topping follows instructions

Instructions

Cream butter & sugar; beat in egg. Add dry ingredients & zest, alternating with milk; stir in vanilla. Spread in greased 9" baking pan. Sprinkle with blueberries & Crumb Topping. Bake 350 F for 45 to 50 min. Crumb Topping: 1/2 cup sugar, 1/3 cup flour , 1/2 tsp cinnamon, 1/4 cup butter, softened. Mix all ingredients until crumbly.
