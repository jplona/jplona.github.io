MINIATURE CHEESECAKES

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERTS
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
24

Ingredients

  • 24 cupcake liners ( 2 1/2")
  • 24 Vanilla Wafers
  • 16 oz. Cream Cheese, softened
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 20 oz can Cherry or Blueberry Filling
  • 1/2 cup sliced Nuts, optional
  • Option: Use 48 (1 3/4") liners for
  • Bon Bons

Instructions

Line cupcake pans with liners. Place a vanilla wafer in bottom of each liner. In small bowl beat cream cheese, sugar, eggs, lemon juice & vanilla until light & fluffy. Fill the liners 3/4 full with cheese mixture. Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 mi n. or until set. Top each with spoonful of filling & 1 tsp of nuts. Chill.
