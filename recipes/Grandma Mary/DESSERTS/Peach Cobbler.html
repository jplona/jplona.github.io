PEACH COBBLER

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Peaches
  • 2/3 cup Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • 1/4 tsp Cinnamon
  • DOUGH MIXTURE
  • 1 cup Flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 tbsp Milk
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/3 cup Crisco

Instructions

Arrange peaches in pie dish. Sprinkle with mixture of sugar, flour & cinnamon. For dough mixture: Combine dry ingredients. Add egg, milk & crisco. Drop dough by tablespoon onto peaches. Bake 350F for 25-30 min.
