NO-BAKE CHEESECAKE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 8 OZ Cream Cheese, softened
  • 1/3 cup Sugar
  • 8 oz Cool Whip, thawed
  • 1 - 9" Pie Crust , ready to use

Instructions

Mix cream cheese & sugar with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended. Gently stir in whipped topping. Spoon into crust. Refrigerate 3 hrs or overnight. Top with fresh fruit or cherry pie filling.
