CREPES

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERTS
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
16 - 6"

Ingredients

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 1/4 cups Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tbsp Butter or Margarine, melted
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Filling: Use Preserves, cottage cheese, etc.
  • Cherries, etc

Instructions

Combine flour, salt, & milk, beating at medium speed of electric mixer until smooth. Add eggs & beat well; stir in melted butter. Refrigerate batter 1 hr. Brush bottom of 6" crepe pan with oil.Heat pan until hot. Pour 2 tbsp batter into pan, tilting p an in all directions to spread batter. Cook 1 min. Flip crepe & cook other side 30 seconds. Place crepes on towel & allow to cool.
