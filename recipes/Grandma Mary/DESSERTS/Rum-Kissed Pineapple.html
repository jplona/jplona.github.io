RUM-KISSED PINEAPPLE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
15 min
Servings
4

Ingredients

  • 1 Pineapple, peeled & cored
  • into 1" chunks
  • 2 tbsp Dark Rum
  • 1 Lime, juice & zest
  • 2 tbsp Brown Sugar, or to taste

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in bowl & macerate at least one hour. Dessert may be prepared a day ahead of time, covered & refrigerated overnight. For best flavor, remove from refrig about 1 hr. before serving. Good with plain cake or ice cream.
