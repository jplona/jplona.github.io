RAINBOW 7 LAYER DESSERT

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
2 hrs
Servings
12

Ingredients

  • 4 - 3oz pkgs Jello - red, green,
  • yellow & orange
  • 4 cup Hot Water
  • 2 cup Cold Water
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 2 envelopes unflavored Gelatin
  • 1/2 cup Cold Water
  • 1 pt Sour Cream
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • Dissolve 1st pkg in 1 cup hot water.
  • Add 1/2 c cold water. Pour into 9x13

Instructions

glass dish & refrig. until firm., about 30 min. Meanwhile, bring milk to a boil, add sugar until dissolved. Remove from heat. Soften unflavored gel in 1/2 c cold water. Mix well & add to hot milk mixture. Add sour cream & vanilla. Beat until smooth. Pour 1/3 over 1 1/2 c of firm Jello in pan. Refrig. until cream layer is firm. Keep remaining cream mixture at room temp. Repeat with remaining Jellos.
