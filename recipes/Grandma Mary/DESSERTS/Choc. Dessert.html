CHOCOLATE DESSERT

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
12

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Flour
  • 3/4 Stick Margarine, softened
  • 1/2 cup Nuts, ground
  • 8 oz Cream Cheese
  • 1 cup Confectioner's Sugar
  • 1 cup Cool Whip
  • 2 sml. pkgs Instant Pudding
  • 3 cups Milk
  • TOPPING: Cool Whip & ground Nuts

Instructions

Mix flour, margarine & nuts as for pie dough. Pat into 9 x 12 pyrex dish. Bake 350F for 15 min or until golden brown. Cool well. With beater, blend cream cheese, confectioner's sugar & cool whip. Spread over cooled crust. Beat milk & pudding. Let set for 10 min. Spread over cheese mixture. Refrigerate. Add topping 1 hr before serving. Filling Options: Lemon or Banana Cream
