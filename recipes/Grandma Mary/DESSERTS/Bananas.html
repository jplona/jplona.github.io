BANANAS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
4

Ingredients

  • 3 lg or 4 sml firm but ripe Bananas
  • 2 1/2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 1/2 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp grated Nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup Vanilla Yogurt

Instructions

Peel bananas & cut into long diagonal slices about 1/2" thick. In large skillet, cook the butter & brown sugar over medium heat, stirring, until sugar dissolves. Place bananas in syrup in a single layer, reduce the heat to med-low & cook 3 to 4 min. u ntil browned. Turn with a spatula & cook on the second side until bananas are soft & richly browned.Top with yogurt & nutmeg. Serve warm.
