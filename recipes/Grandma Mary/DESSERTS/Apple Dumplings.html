APPLE DUMPLINGS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
15 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 2/3 cup Crisco
  • 5 tbsp Water
  • FILLING
  • 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup flour,
  • 1/4 tsp Cinnamon, 6 Granny Smith
  • Apples, Butter
  • SYRUP
  • 1 Cup Sugar, 2 cups Water
  • 4 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon

Instructions

Mix flour & salt. Cut in Crisco. Add water & shape into ball. Roll into 12 x 18" rectangle. Cut into 6 equal squares. Mix sugar, flour & cinnamon. Place 1 apple, 2 tbsp sugar mixture & small piece of butter on each dough square. Bring corners of dou gh up around apple & pinch together edges. Place in 8 x 12" pan. Combine syrup ingredients & boil until sugar dissolves. Cover dumplings with syrup & bake at 350 F for 1 hour.
