STRAWBERRY ANGEL DESSERT

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
30 min.
Servings
12-15

Ingredients

  • 1 sml Angel Cake, broken into pieces
  • 1 lg Strawberry Jello
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 - 10 oz pkgs frozen Strawberries
  • 1 lg pkg Dream Whip or Cool Whip

Instructions

Combine Jello, boiling water & frozen strawberries. Let gel slightly. When set, place broken pieces of angel cake in a 9 x 13 pan. Pour jello mixture over cake. Cover with Dream Whip or Cool Whip.
