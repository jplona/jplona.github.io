RHUBARB CRISP

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
DESSERT
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 6 cups Rhubarb, diced
  • 1/4 cup Sugar, granulated
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar, packed
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Quick Oats, uncooked
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon or apple pie spice
  • 5 tbsp Margarine, room temp.

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350F. Place rhubarb in 9 x 9" baking pan. Sprinkle with granulated sugar. In small bowl, combine brown sugar, flour, oats & spice. Mix well. With pastry blender, cut margarine in until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle over sweetened rhubarb. Bake for 35 min or until golden brown. Serve with vanilla ice cream, milk or vanilla yogurt.
