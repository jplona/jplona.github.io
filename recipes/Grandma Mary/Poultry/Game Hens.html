GAME HENS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
POULTRY
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
4

Ingredients

  • 2 Cornish Hens, 1 1/2# each
  • 4 tsp Olive Oil
  • 2 tbsp Orange Marmalade
  • 1/4 tsp Curry Powder
  • 1 tsp Vinegar, cider or white
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper

Instructions

Preheat broiler. Cut hens in half, discarding backbones. To make glaze, combine 2 tsp of oil , the marmalade & curry powder. Cook over medium heat about 1 min. Stir in vinegar. Brush hens with remaining 2 tsp oil, salt & pepper. Place hens, skin side dow n & broil about 5" from heat source for 10 min. Brush with glaze & turn skin side up. Broil 5 min more until brown.
