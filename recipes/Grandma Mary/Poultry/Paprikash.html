PAPRIKASH

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
POULTRY
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Chicken Breasts, boneless
  • 1 med Onion, halved & sliced
  • 2 tsp Vegetable Oil
  • 2 tsp Paprika
  • 1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 1/4 cup lowfat Sour Cream or Yogurt
  • Hot Cooked Noodles

Instructions

In large skillet, saute chicken & onion in oil until chicken is browned. Sprinkle with paprika. Stir in soup & water. Simmer 5 minutes. Stir in sour cream. Heat. Do not boil. Serve over noodles. Makes about five cups.
