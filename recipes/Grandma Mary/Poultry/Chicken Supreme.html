CHICKEN SUPREME

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
POULTRY
Preparation time
25 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 6 Chicken Breasts, boned & skinned
  • 1 can Mushroom Soup
  • 1 Sml Can Mushrooms with juice
  • 1 cup Sour Cream
  • Salt & Pepper, Garlic Salt
  • Hot Sauce to taste

Instructions

Season chicken with salt, pepper, garlic salt & a few dashes of hot sauce. Mix all ingredients together. Pour over chicken in flat casserole. Bake at 350F for 1 1/2 hrs. Serve over noodles or rice.
