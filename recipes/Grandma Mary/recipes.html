RED CABBAGE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLE
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
4 - 6

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 500 mg Vitamin C, pulverized
  • 1/3 cup Cider Vinegar
  • 1/3 cup Brown Sugar
  • 3 - 4 Whole Cloves
  • 4 - 6 cups finely shredded Cabbage
  • (about 1/2 large head)

Instructions

Mix all ingredients except cabbage in saucepan & simmer 1 minute. Add cabbage & simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally until cabbage pieces are tender & a uniform raspberry color.

POTATOES RED-HOTS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLE
Preparation time
40 min
Servings
4 - 6

Ingredients

  • 3 # little Red Potatoes
  • 1/3 cup extra-virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 tbsp prepared grainy Mustard
  • 1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Instructions

Heat oven 375F. Halve or quarter potatoes.Place in roasting pan. In small bowl combine oil, mustard, cayenne pepper & salt. Toss potatoes with 2/3 of the mixture. Bake uncovered 30 - 40 min, or until tender & browned. Toss potatoes with remaining mix ture & warm thoroughly in oven.

SCALLOPED HAM & POTATOES

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLES
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 4 cups heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 cloves Garlic, slivered
  • 1 tsp dried Thyme leaves
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper
  • 1 # Ham, sliced
  • 2 # Potatoes, peeled & thinly sliced
  • 1 lg Onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp fresh Parsley, chopped

Instructions

Combine cream, garlic, thyme, salt & pepper in heavy saucepan. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer & cook 10 to 12 min until sauce has reduced by third. Butter shallow 8 x 14 pyrex dish. Layer 1/2 of ham, 1/2 of potatoes, 1/2 of onion .Pour 1/2 of cream mi xture over layers. Repeat layering. Bake 350F for 1 hr until brown & bubbly.

ZUCCHINI POTATO PANCAKES

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
VEGETABLE
Preparation time
15 min
Servings
3 - 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Zucchini, grated & unpeeled
  • 2 med Potatoes, peeled & grated
  • 1 tbsp Onion, finely minced
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • Dash of Pepper

Instructions

Stir vegetables into mixture of beaten eggs, salt & pepper. Lightly oil a griddle or skillet. Use 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Flatten the batter with a fork & fry until brown & crisp, about 5 min on each side. Makes 8 to 10 pancakes.
