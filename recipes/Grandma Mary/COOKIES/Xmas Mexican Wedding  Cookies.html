MEXICAN WEDDING COOKIES

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
COOKIES
Preparation time
30 Min.
Servings
Yield 36

Ingredients

  • 1cup Butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup Conf. sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1cup pecans, ground
  • 2 tsp. Vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp Salt

Instructions

Add flour, pecans & vanilla. Shape into walnut size balls. Place on ungreased cookie sheet 1" apart. Flatten cookie with dampened flat glass bottom. Bake approx. 15 min. at 325 degrees F. Coat with conf. sugar.
