CHOCOLATLE MELT-AWAYS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
COOKIES
Preparation time
20 min
Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Butter, softened
  • 1 1/4 cup Conf. Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 cup siftted flour, ( add 2 tbsp flour
  • if necessary)
  • 2 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Nuts, chopped
  • 9 3/4 oz Milk Choc. Bar, melted

Instructions

Cream butter & add sugar gradually. Add vanilla. Sift flour, cornstarch & salt. Add to butter & mix. Fold in nuts & chocolate. Shape into balls (1 tsp full). Bake on ungreased cookie sheet, allowing room for spreading. Bake 250 F for 40 minutes.
