SUGAR CRESCENTS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
COOKIES
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
7 doz.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 # Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 # Margarine
  • 2 1/2 cup Flour
  • 1 cup ground walnuts
  • Pinch Salt
  • Granulated Sugar

Instructions

Mix together all ingredients. Refrigerate dough overnight. Form balls from 1 tsp of batter. Roll into crescent. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 400F for 10 - 12 min. While still warm, roll in granulated sugar.
