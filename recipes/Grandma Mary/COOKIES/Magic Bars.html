MAGIC COOKIE BARS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
COOKIES
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
24

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Margarine or Butter
  • 1 1/2 cups Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • 14 oz Sweet Condensed Milk
  • 1 cup Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • 1 1/2 cups flaked Coconut
  • 1 cup chopped Nuts

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 F (325 F for glass dish) In a 13x9" baking pan, melt margarine in oven. Sprinkle crumbs over margarine. Pour condensed milk evenly over crumbs. Top with remaining ingredients, press down firmly. Bake 25 to 30 min. SEVEN LAYER MAGI C COOKIE BARS: Add 6 oz butterscotch chips after chocolate chips.
