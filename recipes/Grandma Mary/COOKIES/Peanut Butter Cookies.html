Peanut Butter Cookies

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
COOKIES
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
5 doz

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Crisco
  • 1 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 2 cups Peanut Butter
  • 2 Eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup Boiling Water
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • Recipe from: Helen Casey

Instructions

Cream crisco. Add brown & granulated sugars. Add Eggs, vanilla & Peanut Butter. Add in dry ingredients and boiling water. Form into 1" balls. Space 2" apart on baking sheet. With fork, form a criss-cross on each cookie. Bake about 12 min @ 375 F.
