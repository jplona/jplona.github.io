WALNUT BALLS

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
COOKIES
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
5 doz

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Butter
  • 1/4 cup Conf. Sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp Water
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 cup Walnuts, finely chopped

Instructions

Cream butter & sugar. Add all ingredients & mix well. Shape into balls 1/2" in diameter. Bake 375 F for 15 min. Roll in powdered sugar while warm, then again after cool. Yield: approx. 5 do
