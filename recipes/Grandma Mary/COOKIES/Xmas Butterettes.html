BUTTERETTES

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
COOKIES
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
4 doz

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 sticks Butter, softened
  • 2/3 cup Conf. Sugar
  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 2 c Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Almond Extract

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together. Put thru press. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet @ 350F 10-12 min or until bottoms are golden brown. Yield: 4 dozen
