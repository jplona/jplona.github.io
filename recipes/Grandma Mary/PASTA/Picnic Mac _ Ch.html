PICNIC MACARONI & CHEESE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PASTA
Preparation time
20 MIN
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 1# Farfalle, cooked & drained
  • 2 cups X Sharp Cheddar Cheese,Cube
  • 6 or 7 Green Onions, chopped ------
  • discard most of green tops
  • 2-3 tbsp fresh Basil, shredded
  • 1 to 1 1/2 cups tomatoes, diced
  • 4 tbsp fresh Lemon juice
  • 4 tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
  • 2/3 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1/4 to 1/2 tsp Garlic, granulated
  • 1/2 to 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 4 tbsp Dijon Mustard

Instructions

Combine pasta, cheese, onions, tomato & basil in lg bowl. In a separate bowl whisk the mustard, lemon juice, vinegar, olive oil, garlic & onion powder. Add to pasta mixture; toss well. Season to taste with salt & pepper. Cover & refrigerate overnight , tossing occasionally. Serve chilled.
