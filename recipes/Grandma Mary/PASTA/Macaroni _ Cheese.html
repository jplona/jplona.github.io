Instructions

scalded milk. Stir constantly until cheese melts. Add salt and pepper. Cook macaroni 5 to 10 min. Drain & rinse. Alternate layers of macaroni & cheese sauce in a buttered 1 1/2 qt. casserole. Sprinkle with remaining grated cheese, bread crumbs & paprika . Bake uncovered 20-25 min until crumbs are browned. Note: To scald milk, heat it slowly in a small pan until tiny bubbles appear around the edge, but before the milk boils up.
