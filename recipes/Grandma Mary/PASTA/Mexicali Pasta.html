MEXICALI PASTA

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PASTA
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Sea-Shell Macaroni ---------
  • (about 3 l/2 c), cooked & drained
  • 1 1/4 cups prepared Salsa
  • 16 oz can Black Beans, rinse & drain
  • 1 pkg (10 oz) frozen corn, thawed

Instructions

In large bowl conbine sea-shells, salsa, beans & corn. Serve at room temperature. NOTE: to serve hot, combine salsa, beans & corn in large skillet; heat through. Toss with hot sea-shells.
