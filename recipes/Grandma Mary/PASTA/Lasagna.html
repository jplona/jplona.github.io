LASAGNA

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PASTA
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cup Spaghetti Sauce, (28 oz)
  • 1# Ground Beef, cooked & drained
  • 2 cups Cottage Cheese
  • 1 Egg, beaten
  • 6 Lasagna Noodles, cooked --------
  • drained & rinsed
  • 2 cups Kraft Mozzarella Cheese -----
  • Shredded Low-Moisture Part-Skim ---
  • (divided)

Instructions

Mix sauce & cooked meat. Set aside. Mix cottage cheese & egg until blended. Spoon 1 c of sauce mixture on bottom of 12x8 baking dish. Layer half of lasagna noodles, half of cottage cheese mixture, one-third of cheese & half of remaining sauce mixture in d ish. Repeat layers, ending with sauce. Spread with remaining shredded cheese. Bake 350F for 45 min. Let stand 10 min before serving.
