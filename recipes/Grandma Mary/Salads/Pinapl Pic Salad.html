PINEAPPLE PICNIC SALAD

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SALAD
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 20 oz Crushed Pineapple, undrained
  • 8 oz Cream Cheese, softened
  • 1 lg pkg Instant Vanilla Pudding Mix
  • 12 oz Pineapple chunks, drained
  • 8 oz Cool Whip, thawed

Instructions

Gradually stir undrained pineapple into softened cream cheese. Stir in pudding mix. When it starts to thicken fold in pineapple chunks & whipped topping. Pour into 9 x 13" dish. Chill. Pie Filling: Gently fold 1 to 2 sml bananas, sliced into mixture. Spoon into two 8" ready to use graham cracker pie crusts. Smooth mixtue. Garnish with blueberries or strawberries. Chill overnight.
