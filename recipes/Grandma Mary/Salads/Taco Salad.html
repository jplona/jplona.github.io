TACO SALAD

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SALAD
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 1 Head Lettuce, shred as for coleslaw
  • 1 # Grd Chuck, brown & crumble
  • 1 med Onion, chopped
  • 1 Green Pepper, chopped
  • 1 # Sharp Chedar Cheese, shredded
  • 2 Tomatoes, cubed
  • 1 10 oz bag Taco Chips, crushed
  • 4 Scallions, chopped
  • 1 - 8oz Catalina Dressing

Instructions

Brown meat & onion. Drain off excess fat. Add remaining ingredients. Toss with Catalina dressing. Optional - Add one bottle of Mild Salsa or Mild Sauce (Ortega) to browned meat if desired. Use more meat if desired.
