SEVEN LAYER SALAD

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SALAD
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
10

Ingredients

  • 1 head Lettuce, shredded (1/2 Bowl)
  • 1/2 cup Green Pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Celery, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Scallions, chopped
  • 1 box frozen peas, cook & drain
  • 1 1/2 cups Hellman's Salad Dressing
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 8 slices Bacon, cook & crumble
  • 1 cup Cheese, shredded (use Swiss
  • or Cheddar)

Instructions

In large bowl layer lettuce, green pepper, celery, scallions & peas. Spread salad dressing over top. Sprinkle with sugar & bacon. Add cheese. Cover with saran wrap. Refrigerate for at least 24 hours. Toss when ready to serve.
