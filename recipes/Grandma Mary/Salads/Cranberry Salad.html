CRANBERRY SALAD

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SALAD
Preparation time
15 min
Servings
10

Ingredients

  • 3 envelopes Unflavored Gelatine
  • 3/4 cup Cold Water
  • 4 cups Cranberry Juice - divided
  • 1/3 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/3 cup Lemon Juice
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped unpeeled Apple
  • 3/4 cup chopped Celery
  • 1/2 cup broken Walnuts
  • Dressing : equal parts of mayo
  • and sour cream

Instructions

In a large bowl soften gelatine in cold water. Heat 1 1/2 cups juice, add sugar & salt to gelatin & stir until dissolved. Add lemon juice & remaining 2 1/2 cups cranberry juice. Pour 3/4 cups in 1 1/2 qt mold. Chill until firm. Chill remaining gela tine until it begins to set, then fold in apple,celery & walnuts. Put in mold & chill overnight. Serve with dressing.
