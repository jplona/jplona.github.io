LAYERED SALAD

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SALAD
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 1 Hd Lettuce, or any combination
  • 1/3 cup Green Onion, chopped
  • 8 oz Green Peas, frozen
  • 8 oz Water Chestnuts, sliced
  • 1 1/2 cup Mayonaise, Hellman's
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Seasoned Salt
  • Pepper to taste

Instructions

Shred lettuce into a large bowl. Place onion, peas, and water chestnuts over lettuce. Spread mayo over top. Sprinkle on mixture of Cheese, garlic powder, salt & pepper. Before serving arrange top of salad with tomatoes, eggs, & bacon bits.
