SINFUL SALAD

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SALAD
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
12

Ingredients

  • 6 oz pkg Strawberry Jello
  • 2 cups Boiling Water
  • 3 med Bananas
  • 20 oz Strawberries, thaw & drain
  • 20 oz Crushed Pineapple, drained
  • 1 pt Sour Cream

Instructions

Combine Jello & boiling water until completely dissolved. Cool. Add bananas, strawberries & pineapple to Jello. Divide in half. Pour half of Jello mixture into 8" x 12" pan & refrigerate until set. (about 1 hr) Keep remaining Jello at room temp. Spread sour cream evenly over set Jello in pan. Pour on remaining Jello. Cover & refrigerate until set. About 1 1/2 hrs or overnight.
