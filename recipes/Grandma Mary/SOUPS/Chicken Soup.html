CHICKEN SOUP

From the kitchen of Mom Plona's Recipe

Category
SOUP
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
8 - 10

Ingredients

  • 4 to 5# Chicken
  • 16 cups Water
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 12 Peppercorns
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 1 med. Onion, with skin
  • 1 to 2 Chicken Bouillon Cubes
  • 3 ribs Celery, with leaves
  • Parsley leaves , sml bunch
  • 3 Potatoes, 6 Carrots, 1 Parsley Root

Instructions

Place chicken & water into stock pot.Bring to hard boil. Skim surface. Add salt, peppercorns, garlic, whole onion , bouillon cubes, parsley & celery. Simmer slowly for about three hours. Chicken should almost fall apart. Add whole potatoes, carrots & parsley root last 30 min of cooking. Remove chicken & vegetables. Strain soup if desired.
