PUMPKIN SOUP

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SOUP
Preparation time
15 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Butter or Margarine
  • 3/4 cup Onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cups Chicken Stock
  • 2 cups Cooked Pumpkin
  • from 2 1/2# Pumpkin
  • 1 cup Half & Half
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1/8 tsp Chilli Powder
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 F. Remove seeds & halve pumpkin. Bake a 2 1/2# pumpkin with skin side up on oiled baking sheet until skin is wrinkled & flesh is soft, about 1 hr. Cool, peel off skin. Saute onion in butter until golden. Stir in all remaining ingre dients except Half & Half. Simmer 15 min. Puree mixture in blender. Return to pan & add Half & Half. Do not boil. Serve with dollop of sour cream.
