CARROT SOUP

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SOUP
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 20 Carrots, medium size
  • Chicken Broth
  • 3 Onions, peeled & diced
  • 3 Bay Leaves
  • 2 tbsp Canola Oil
  • Salt, Pepper to taste

Instructions

Trim & halve unpeeled carrots. Place in pot & add broth to cover. Bring to a boil, lower heat & simmer until carrots are tender, about 40 min. Saute onions & bay leaves in oil until onion is translucent. Place carrots & onions in blender; remove bay leaves. Puree until smooth, adding cooking liquid from carrots as needed for consistency. Add salt & pepper.
