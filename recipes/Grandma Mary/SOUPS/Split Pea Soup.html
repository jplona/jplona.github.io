SPLIT PEA SOUP

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
SOUP
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
6

Ingredients

  • 16 oz Pkg Green Split Peas
  • 3 qts Water
  • 1 sml Ham Shank or Hock ( 1 1/2#)
  • 1 lg Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 Chicken Bouillon Cubes
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Oregano Leaves
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1 1/2 cup Carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup Celery, chopped

Instructions

In deep pot combine peas, water, ham shank, onion, chicken bouillon & seasonings. Simmer uncovered 1 1/2 hrs. Remove ham shank, trim meat off bone & return to pot. Stir in carrots & celery. Simmer uncovered 1 1/2 to 2 hrs or until soup reaches desire d thickness.
