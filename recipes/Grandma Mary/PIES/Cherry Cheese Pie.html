CHERRY CHEESE PIE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PIE
Preparation time
25 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 1 (9") Graham Cracker Crust or
  • baked pastry shell
  • 8 oz Cream Cheese, softened
  • 14 oz Sweet Condensed Milk
  • 1/3 cup ReaLemon Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 21 oz can Cherry Pie Filling, chilled

Instructions

In larlge mixer bowl, beat cheese until fluffy. Gradually beat in sweetened condensed milk until smooth. Stir in ReaLemon brand & vanilla. Pour into prepared crust. Chill 3 hrs or until set. Top with desired amount of pie filling before serving.
