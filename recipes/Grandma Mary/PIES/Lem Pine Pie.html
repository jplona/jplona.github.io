LEMON-PINEAPPLE PIE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PIE
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 1 9" Pie Crust, baked
  • 1 can Thank You Lemon Filling
  • 20 oz Crushed Pineapple, undrained
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 box Dream Whip (2 envelope pkg)

Instructions

Mix lemon filling & crushed pineapple. Beat dream whip (2 envelopes) per directions.. Fold into filling. Add vanilla. Pour into baked pie crust. Chill in refrigerator over night.Top with cool whip.
