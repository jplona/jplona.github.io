CHOCOLATE VELVET PIE

From the kitchen of Mary C Plona

Category
PIE
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 1 - 9" unbaked Pie Crust
  • 2 - 1 oz squares unsweetened Choc.
  • 1 - 14 oz Sweet Condensed Milk
  • 2 Eggs, well beaten
  • 1 cup Water
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • Whipped Cream

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400F. Bake pie crust 15 min. In heavy saucepan, over low heat, melt choc. with sweetened condensed milk. Remove from heat. Stir in eggs. Add water & vanilla. Mix well. Pour into hot pie crust. Bake 10 min. Reduce oven temp. to 300F. Bake 20 min longer or until center is set. Cool . Top with whipped cream.
