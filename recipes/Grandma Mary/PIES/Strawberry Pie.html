STRAWBERRY PIE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PIE
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 9" Pie Crust, baked & cooled
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1//4 cup Cornstarch
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 1/2 cup Water
  • 1 pkg. Strawberry Jello
  • 1 Qt. Strawberries, hull & wash
  • 1/2 pt Whipped Cream

Instructions

Combine water, sugar, salt & cornstarch. Cook until clear. Stir in Jello. Cool. Place one half of berries in pastry shell, one half in cooled mixture. Add remaining mixture in pie crust. Top with whipped cream
