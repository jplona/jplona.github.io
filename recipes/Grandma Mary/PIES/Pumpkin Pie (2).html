PUMPKIN PIE (2)

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PIE
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1 can Pumpkin solid pack (29 0z)
  • 1 1/2 cups Sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Cinnamon, ground
  • 1 tsp Ginger, ground
  • 1/2 tsp Cloves, ground
  • 3 cups Evaporated Milk
  • 2 9" unbaked Pie Crusts

Instructions

Preheat oven to 425F. Combine filling in order given; pour into pie shells Bake 14 min. Reduce temp to 350F. Bake additional 45 min or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Cool. Garnish with whipped topping. Makes 2 9" pies.
