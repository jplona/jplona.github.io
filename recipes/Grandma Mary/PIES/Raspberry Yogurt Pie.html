RASPBERRY YOGURT PIE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PIE
Preparation time
15 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 1 Keebler Ready Crust Shortbread
  • pie crust
  • 1 carton (10 oz) frozen Red
  • Raspberries, thawed
  • 2/3 cup Sugar
  • 2 Envelopes (2 tbsp) unflavored
  • Gelatin
  • 1 carton (8 oz) Vanilla Yogurt
  • 1 cup Sour Cream

Instructions

Drain raspberries, reserving juice. Add enough water to juice to measure 2/3 cup. In saucepan, combine sugar & gelatin; add reserved liquid. Heat & stir until gelatin & sugar dissolve. Remove from heat. Gradually stir in yogurt & sour cream; mix well. Stir in raspberries. Pour into crust. Chill until set, about 4 hours. Garnish with whipped topping as desired.
