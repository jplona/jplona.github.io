Cheese Bon Bons

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PIES
Preparation time
30 min
Servings
36

Ingredients

  • 2 - 8 oz pkgs Cream Cheese
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • Crushed Vanilla Wafers

Instructions

Place 1 tsp crushed wafers in bon bon cups. With electric mixer beat remaining ingredients until smooth. Fill cups with mixture almost to top. Bake 350F for 20 min. Optional: Place cherry pie filling on each bon bon.
