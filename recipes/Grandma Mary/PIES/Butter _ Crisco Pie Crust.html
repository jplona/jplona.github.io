BUTTER & CRISCO PIE CRUST (2)

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PIE
Preparation time
15 min
Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups Flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted, cold Butter
  • 1/2 cup Crisco
  • 6 - 7 tbsp Cold Water

Instructions

Mix all ingredients with pastry blender. Roll out dough and chill 1/2 hour. Add filling & bake per pie recipe.
