BANANA CREAM PIE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PIE
Preparation time
20 min
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 9" baked Pie Crust
  • 3 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1 2/3 cups Water
  • 14 oz Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 3 Egg Yolks, beaten
  • 2 tbsp Butter or Margarine
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 3 medium Bananas
  • ReaLemon Juice Concentrate
  • Whipped Cream

Instructions

In heavy saucepan, dissolve cornstarch in water; stir in sweetened condensed milk & egg yolks. Cook & stir until thickened & bubbly. Remove from heat; add margarine & vanilla. Cool slightly. Slice 2 bananas; dip in ReaLemon & drain. Arrange on botto m of prepared crust. Pour filling over bananas; cover. Chill 4 hrs or until set. Spread top with whipped cream. Slice remaining banana; dip in ReaLemon & garnish top of pie.
