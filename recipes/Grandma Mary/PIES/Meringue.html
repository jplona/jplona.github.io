NEVER FAIL MERINGUE

From the kitchen of Mary C. Plona

Category
PIE
Preparation time
20 min
Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 6 tbsp White Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 3 Egg Whites
  • Few grains Salt

Instructions

Mix cornstarch, sugar, water & salt & cook thick & clear. Cool slightly. Beat egg whites until frothy. Continue beating while slowly pouring cooked mixture over, and continue beating 5 minutes. Cover cooled filling in pie shell with meringue sealing e dges to crust. Bake at 450 F for 5 to 7 min until golden.
